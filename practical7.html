Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was a great scientist and the 11th President of India. He is known as the "Missile Man of India". He inspired millions of students with his thoughts and dedication.

His contributions in science and technology are remarkable. He always encouraged young minds to dream big and work hard to achieve their goals.
